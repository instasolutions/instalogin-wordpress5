Official Instalogin integration enables login via InstaCode on your Wordpress installation.

== Installation ==

Upon activating the plugin you will be taken to the installation wizard.
Alternatively you may navigate to the Instalogin settings in the backend.
You will be required to provide a key and a secret to connect to the Instalogin servers.
A key pair may be acquired at https://instalogin.me/product-category/licence/
